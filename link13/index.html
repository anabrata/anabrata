Drive URL to Box Viewer

Tip: Set the file’s sharing to “Anyone with the link → Viewer” in Google Drive so it can load publicly.
Works with typical links: https://drive.google.com/file/d/FILE_ID/view, https://drive.google.com/open?id=FILE_ID, or a raw FILE_ID.
Paste a Google Drive link or ID, pick a mode, then press “Show in Box”.
